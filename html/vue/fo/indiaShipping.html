Shipping Policy

(v20200710.1.1)

rNetwork India Pvt. Ltd. has an objective to provide the highest level of satisfaction to our Customers. We ensure that the Customer experience post purchase is smooth, seamless and delightful with regards to the quality and speed of delivery. We also keep our Customers appraised about various stages of the order placed: Order Processing, Dispatch & Delivery.

How much does it cost to ship my product?

  • The cost of shipping depends on the product purchased as well as the location the product is being shipped to. Please be sure to verify all associated shipping costs at the time of purchase.

How long before my products are shipped and delivered?

  • Each order purchased directly from rNetwork will be delivered within 7-10 days from the date of order confirmation, given that the delivery is not delayed due to concerns raised by any governmental authority or any other entity acting on behalf of the government or acting as per the directions of the government. If in case the delivery period exceeds the stipulated time, either due to mishap or unforeseen circumstances, the Customer is notified regarding the same.
